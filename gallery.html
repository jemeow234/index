Philippines Landscape




I’m passionate about exploring the stunning landscapes of the Philippines,
from pristine beaches to scenic mountains and rice terraces.
Capturing these natural wonders through photography inspires me
and deepens my appreciation for the country’s beauty and rich culture.







Foreign Landscape




I’m fascinated by the diverse landscapes of other countries,
from majestic mountains and vast deserts to serene lakes and vibrant cities.
Exploring these places allows me to experience different cultures
and capture their beauty through photography. It inspires me to see
the world from new perspectives and appreciate the uniqueness of each destination.
